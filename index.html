Logomark Usage
Color by Background
White background

Incorrect Uses — Never Do This
1.Add patterns to the logomark
2.Place inside a solid shape
3.Place inside outlined boxes or frames
4.Use outlined (hollow) versions — must always be solid-filled
5.Add shadows, glows, or special effects
6.Stretch, squish, or distort in any way
7.Use faded/light version on white — use solid black, maroon, or white only
8.Use screened (faded) version on dark background — white only on dark
9.Rotate vertically — must always sit horizontally
Ursa Minor Bear Symbol
🐻
PBL's official secondary symbol — the Ursa Minor Bear. Connected to the North Star (Polaris), symbolizing guidance, direction, and ambition.
Core Values Embodied
Inclusion
Integrity
Curiosity
Courage
Collaboration
Meaning
The Ursa Minor constellation is home to Polaris (the North Star). Polaris Research Group resides within PBL (Ursa Minor) — always learning, following professional guidance, while standing out individually.
Usage Rules
· Use maroon bear on white, or white bear on maroon
· Never alter the proportions
· Artwork may be reduced/enlarged but must retain original proportions
· 4 bear style variants — choose based on context
Publications Guide
Instagram
2 fonts max per cover. Include Φ logomark + PBL ID. Bold headlines, minimal noise. Reels: creative discretion — no explicit content.
Posters & Flyers
2 fonts max. Always include Φ logomark. QR codes acceptable. Orange tertiary color allowed when it enhances design.
LinkedIn
Maroon banner, white Φ logo. Tagline: "Cultivating Industry Professionals at De Anza College." Professional tone, text-heavy, credit contributors.
Newsletter
Bold masthead, 1 typeface. Prominent Φ logomark. Use Mailchimp. Bold headlines, bullet points, embedded hyperlinks. Include event schedules + links.
Paper & Format: Vertical layout standard. High-quality non-absorbent paper. Avoid pale/pastel colors. 1-color printing: use medium-to-dark tones only.

· Proper grammar, punctuation, capitalization
· Flush-left alignment, line-spacing between paragraphs (no indents)
